Hi, I'm Lewis. I am an upcoming PhD graduate in Statistics (December 2021) from Colorado School of Mines, USA. I develop spatial statistics tool to analyze data sets on the order of tens-of-millions of observations. A primary component of my PhD focus has been as a Research Assistant at the National Center for Atmospheric Research (NCAR) in Boulder, Colorado, USA.
