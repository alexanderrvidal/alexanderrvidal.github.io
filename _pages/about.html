Hi, I'm Alex. I recently completed my PhD at Colorado School of Mines in the Department of Applied Mathematics and Statistics, where I studied under the supervision of Dr. Samy Wu Fung and Dr. Luis Tenorio.

Currently, I am a senior data scientist at NerdWallet after surviving a successful exit from fintech startup, On The Barrelhead. Prior to that, I worked at a variety of engineering companies working on practical design problems in engineering and control.

My research lies in the intersection of deep learning with optimal transport and optimal control to aid in solving practical problems in physics, finance, medicine, and data science. In particular, I am broadly interested in optimal transport, optimal control, inverse problems, and deep learning.
